# Beginner Guide

This is a section custom-made so newbies can get the basics down and also not feel lost when they first join the server.

## First steps (10-20 minutes)
When you first join the server, you’ll spawn in a world 1-8 (VIPs have world 9-10) or you'll spawn in the HUB.
### The HUB
The Hub is the main meeting place. Here you can travel between worlds (1-10) and raid worlds (1-4).
 - The main /s market is also located here. If you want to buy or sell things to other players, this is where you'll get it done. 
 - There are also traid windows in the hub for player to player trades.
 - You can enter the HUB with the command /hub or /hub 1-3
 
### Choose your World:
Now you have to choose a world, all worlds use the same map, so the choice doesn't affect gameplay significantly. There you can build your main base. 

### Starter Quests
After joining a world, type /q or /quest to open the quest menu. Start by accepting the Getting Started [1–3] quests. These require collecting 5000 stone, 5000 wood, and 1000 metal ore, rewarding you with early building materials, XP, and RP.

### Build your Base
After completing these starter quests, you should have enough materials to place a small starter base (use /sethome "name" for quick and easy teleport to your base). You don’t need to worry about doors or locks, as only you and your teammates can access what you place—one less thing to worry about. 

### Progressing
Once your base is down, continue progressing through quests. Open the quest menu again and accept Virtual Oil Baron and Barrel Basher, which require collecting 10 diesel and destroying 75 barrels respectively.

After completing these quests, return to the quest menu and claim all the rewards. You should now have enough RP to purchase your own Recycler. This will be very useful later in your wipe; for now, you can use it to recycle unwanted items and unlock useful blueprints. You can also use the command /sell to sell, for example components to the server. This will reward you with enough RP to do other things like raiding etc.

This covers the basics for getting started. In the following pages, you’ll find more information on progression, different playstyles, and tips to help you choose the path that suits you best. 

**TIP:** To get starter guns / explosives, you can join the raid islands by going in the /hub and walking through the red portals named Raid World 1-4, in there you will find 6 different portals named Brad 1-6, join any one of them and look on the ground, usually more experience players leave behind stuff they don’t need!

**TIP2:**  You can use **/kit** to get some starting materials like medical supplies, wood, stone, and so on. If you auth yourself on Discord, you'll get access to more kits.

**TIP3:** With the command **/info** you'll get and overview of all the commands, different activities etc. This is a realy helpfull command you should use.

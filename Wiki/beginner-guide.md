# Beginner Guide

This is a section custom made so newbies can get the basics down and also not feel lost when they first join the server.

## First steps (10-20 minutes)
When you first join the server, you’ll need to choose a Node (World). This is where you will build your main base and store your loot. There are 9 worlds available, all using the same map, so the choice doesn’t affect gameplay significantly.
After joining a world, type /q or /quest to open the quest menu. Start by accepting the Getting Started [1–3] quests. These require collecting 5000 stone, 5000 wood, and 1000 metal ore, rewarding you with early building materials, XP, and RP.

After completing these starter quests, you should have enough materials to place a small starter base. You don’t need to worry about doors or locks, as only you can access what you place—one less thing to worry about. Once your base is down, continue progressing through quests. Open the quest menu again and accept Virtual Oil Baron and Barrel Basher, which require collecting 10 diesel and destroying 75 barrels respectively.

After completing these quests, return to the quest menu and claim all the rewards. You should now have enough RP to purchase your own Recycler. This will be very useful later in your wipe; for now, you can use it to recycle unwanted items and unlock useful blueprints.

This covers the basics for getting started. In the following pages, you’ll find more information on progression, different playstyles, and tips to help you choose the path that suits you best.

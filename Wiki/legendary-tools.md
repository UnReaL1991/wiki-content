# Legendary Tools

Highly recommended for efficiency

## Tool + Effect
Brits Broken Axe - Chance to spawn 4 ores around the player.
Brits Broken Pickaxe - +60% yield from ores.
Fertile Bone - Instantly grows crops at a cost of 50 bone fragments per stage (for each crop, found by taking wild collectables).
Reaping Spade - Harvests all plants in a 6m radius (3x3 planter, found by collecting stone / wood logs on the ground).
Bal0o’s Marrowpaw - Gives 4x bones per animal harvested (found by harvesting bears)

TIP: Use task-specific gear alongside your legendary tools for max efficiency (e.g., Lumberjack set + Brits Broken Axe). You can also drop a Fertile Bone and a Reaping Spade from picking up wild collectibles.

## Armor Inserts
Armor inserts provide additional protection or resistance when added to armor. Each series has multiple tiers (+, ++, +++) with increasing effectiveness.
## Wooden (Satiated Series)
Protects against Bite, Slashes, Blunt, Stabs, Hunger, Thirst and Bee sting. + -> 1.0, ++ -> 2.0, +++ -> 3.0 to all types.
## Asbestos (Waterproof Series)
Protects against Drowning and Cold Exposure.
+ -> 1.0 cold, ++ -> 2.0 cold, +++ -> 3.0 cold.
## Lead (Impact Series)
Protects against Fall, Explosion and Anti-vehicle.
+ -> 1.0, ++ -> 2.0, +++ -> 3.0 to all types.
## Metal (Metal Series)
Protects against Bullet, Bleeding and Explosion.
+ -> bullet 4.0, bleed 2.0 and explosive 2.0
++ -> bullet 6.0, bleed 4.0 and explosive 4.0
+++ -> bullet 8.0, bleed 6.0 and explosive 6.0

TIP: Use series-specific inserts depending on your playstile.
Satiated for survival and PVE hazards, Waterproof for swimming or rainy environments and Impact/Metal for raiding or combat scenarios.

# Best RP Method

## Best Rp Method (Updated)
## This Is Currently The Best Method

## Requirements
- 600+ Cloth seeds
- Reaping Spade
- Fertile Bone
- Full Farming Set
- Mushroom soups (x2)
- 100k+ bone fragments
## Method (step-by-step)
1. Wait for night-time (use /tod to check sunset).
 Night lasts about 4 minutes.
2. Consume Mushroom Soup to increase farming yield.
3. Use the Reaping Spade on the center planter of your 3x3 setup to harvest all crops instantly.
4. Immediately replant the seeds and use Fertile Bone to skip growth.
5. Repeat the process till night ends.
## Converting to RP
1. After night is over, check cloth prices by using /pricecheck cloth.
2. Move to the server that has the highest rates.
3. Sell the cloth (/sell) in the world closest to ~2x RP.

This method is currently one of the fastest and most consistent RP farming strategies on Brits PvE Worlds, especially when combined with a full farming setup and large seed stock.

You could also buy scrap and buy sheets if you don’t want to farm yourself, it's still an optimal method if you sell the sheets at 70 RP.

TIP: You can either farm the bones by using a Hunter Full Set with a Baloo Knife (and a bear pie together with a super serum) or directly buy them by going to the /hub then opening the shop (/s) in the stock market section and looking for bone fragments.

Buy them at a rate of 10/15 RP per, higher than that will not make you have any profits or barely any.

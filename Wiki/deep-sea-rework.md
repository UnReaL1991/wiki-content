## Cargoship Event 1.0
A custom world event that takes place in the Deep Sea. When it spawns, five cargoships appear surrounded by multiple cobalt special forces (boats) guarding the area.

Players must fight through the heavy defenses and board the ships to claim the high-end loot waiting inside. This event is considered one of the most dangerous encounters, but it offers some of the best rewards possible.

TIP: To reach the deep sea, build a boat and head towards the end of the map (if the deep sea is active you will see it shown), the deep sea activates at intervals of 4h (3h open - 1h cooldown).

# Progression & Levels

## Overview
On Brits PvE Worlds, leveling is central to progression. Your level determines access to better skills as each level will give you 2 Skill Points that you can invest in them.
## Important Notes
The XP wipes every 6 months, to keep progression fresh and interesting, also because of updates and META changes, every 100 levels you get to prestige (/pr) which gives you a special reward and the max prestige is Pr10.
## Best Way to Level Up
The fastest way to gain XP is by completing Quartermaster tasks up to T4, which give large amounts of XP without taking much time.
For maximum efficiency, equip your first full legendary set, which varies depending on the task:

## Wood Quartermaster Task
To complete this quest the fastest you will need to be equipped with a Brits Broken Axe (not required but will be helpful in case you want to do all of the quartermaster quests together as it gives you the chance to spawn 4 different nodes around you) and the full Lumberjack set, which will give you a total boost of 100% for your wood yield.

## Stone/Metal/Sulfur Quartermaster Task
As the title explains, for the other 3 tasks you will only need one set as they all fall in the same category as “mining”, first you will need to acquire a Brits Broken Pickaxe (that unlike last time, its now required) and a full Mining Set, which will give you a total boost of 160% for your mining yield.

Special Tip: On top of these sets, you can consume Bangers and Mash for 100% more ore yield or Grilled Pork Kebab for 25% more yield from cutting down trees (you can additionally use a bear pie / super serum on top of these).

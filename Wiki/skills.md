# Skills

Skills (/st) Guide
## Overview
For new players, the Maintenance skill in the Combat section is highly recommended. It prevents gear from taking damage, making it essential early on. Unlocking the right skills early also helps speed up leveling and resource gathering.

## Recommended Early Skills
The most recommended skills are those that help you level up faster so following the prior tab, we will be focussing on the Quartermaster tasks once again so, prioritize unlocking:
- Maintenance(Combat): Prevents tools from taking any damage.
- Mining section: All skills under this tree are equally important, but if you want to focus on yield, then the left side will suffice.
- Woodcutting Section: Like the mining section, all skills under this tree are equally important, but you can focus on the left side.
TIP: Every skill in the Mining and Woodcutting trees is highly recommended for early progression.

## Mid-Game Skills
Once you start tackling Raids, Bradleys, and Helis, focus on the following:
- Drum mag (Combat): fully unlocked, it increases magazine capacity by 50%.
- Raiding section: unlock all skills here; they are essential for raiding and some are useful even against Bradleys.
TIP: If you want to tackle raids its best if you get yourself a Brits Boom Stick, its a revolver that shoots rockets, if you want to focus on bradleys then get yourself an Ashmaker its a revolver that shoots HVs and lastly, if you want to do helicopters then get yourself an M2.

## Late-Game
There isn’t much to say about late game, if you are here it means you already know your preferred playstyle so i am sure you will already have unlocked the skills you are more interested in, what i can suggest is to unlock the Crafting Tree as it helps you with the more demanding quests like Boom for the Boom God.

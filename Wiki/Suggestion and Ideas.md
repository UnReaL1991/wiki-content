# Feedback and Ideas
You can provide feedback or post your suggestions in the Discord channel #suggestions. This channel is the best way to share your thoughts on new content/ideas or feedback on existing content/plugins. But don't tag team members! They will read your post even if they don't reply. 

## How to write a good suggestion
- Don't just jot down your first thought and post it. Think through your idea or feedback thoroughly. 
- Use the search function to find a similar post. If you find one, simply add your thoughts there to keep the channel organized and prevent multiple posts about the same problem/idea. 
- Write constructive posts. Try not only to talk about the problem but also to offer solutions. Or provide a detailed explanation of why your idea would be a good addition to the server and what would change for the better as a result.
- The same applies to feedback on other suggestions. Always be constructive and don't just write "Bad idea". Explain why you consider the idea unsuitable. Or simply suggest a better solution.If you have nothing to say, don't write it down.

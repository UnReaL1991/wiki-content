# Tackling Helis

## Normal Method
### Recommended gear
1. Full HQM Armor Set
2. M249 (M2)
3. Drum Mag skill (from the /st skill tree).
4. Blackberries for quick healing and/or other medicals, tea (Max Health Tea). 
   - These greatly improve survivability and sustained damage when fighting patrol helicopters on Brits PVE Worlds.
### Starting the event
1. Use /tower1-6 in one of the worlds (best one is /tower6).
2. Open the store with /s and head over to Buyable Helis section.
3. Choose a difficulty;
   - Without a Brits Golden M249 or Impact Armor, avoid Elite and Legendary patrols as they deal extremely high damage and can easily one-shot players.

## Combat Strategy
1. Aim for the top rotor and tail rotor for maximum damage.
2. If your aim is strong, use standard 5.56 for better damage output.
3. If you’re less confident, use HV 5.56 and aim at the main body.

**TIP:** Try to land the final shots when the patrol is the closest to the tower so that the crates land on the land around it instead of the water or you will have to swim to them.

## Safety TIPS
- If you struggle to finish heli, ask help in the chat, many players are willing to assist others when needed.
- Be aware that teammates can loot bodies; if things go wrong, gear may be lost.
- If you want to despawn the heli, simply type: /hsretire.

PS: You can also eat a Steak Dinner to reduce the damage taken from the bleeding effect by 100%, a Pumpkin Pie in case of extreme situations (it heals you back to 100% of your health) and finally Gnocchi Neapolitan to increase your max health by 30%.

## Advanced Method
You can build your own heli tower with god peeks like the heli tower 1-6 have and turrets for instant reload of your weapon. 

### How to build your own tower
There are many different ways to build a heli tower, I will show you two different towers. This towers can be included in your main base or be place somewhere else in your home world. 
 - Be aware, to many Helis on the mainland will produce lag for you and other players!
 - With this two designs you can't build higher then 5 floors, stability prevents it!
**TIP:** The higher the foundation starts, the better the visibility. A mountain or hill is well suited for a helicopter landing pad.

### Instant reload feature
Each self-made tower can be upgradet with a turret, that automaticly reloads your M249 (M2). 
  - You only need 1 Turret, a spare M2 and ammunation.
  - The turret doesn't need power!
  - Just put a M2 into the turret and ammunation and it's finished.
  - If you now open the turret and right click on your M2 (in your inventory), it will be replaced with the M2 of the turret with full ammo. You can also drag and drop it, if you have for example, a Golden M2 and only want to use that.

## Tower 1 (step-by-step)
![](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/25e96196-72ce-4467-912c-f2d591ade333) 

*This tower has 8 god peeks 1st floor and 8 air peeks on the 2nd floor.*
**You find each picture (1-6) at the bottom of the topic!**

---

1. Build a 4x4 foundation on the ground floor.
2. Build one wall frame as an entry and 3 walls on the middle square; place the TC in one of the corners.
3. Now build this square 4 floors up. Once you have done this, fill each empty square with wall frames 4 floors up **(step 1)**.
4. Now fill in the floors in the empty spaces. Place 3 ladders inside the entry. You should now have a 4x4 fourth floor with only one empty square where your entry is.
5. Place 4–8 triangle floors (1–2 on each side), depending on how many god peeks you want to have.
6. Now place two half walls on each triangle floor and a wall frame on top of each.
   - Then add a triangle floor on top of the two wall frames.
   - In front of the half walls, add another wall frame and, on top of it, a half wall + low wall.
   - It should now look like **step 2**.
7. Now fill the empty space between the triangle floors with two walls and place windows on each corner. Add the same on top to create a second floor **(step 3)**.
8. Now, in the inner 3x3 area, place 2 windows on each corner and wall frames between the windows. Repeat this a second time on top of each window and wall frame **(step 4)**.
9. Now fill the rest of the empty space with floors, leaving only the middle square empty, and put a floor frame in **(step 5)**.
10. Now you can remove the half walls on the triangle floors and replace them with low walls.
    - Rotate one low wall and upgrade it to sheet metal if you use the container skin. This will give you a flat peek without any extra texture.
    - Also, upgrade the low wall on the second floor to sheet metal.
    - It should look like **step 6** now.
11. Now you can reskin garage doors with a window skin (use /sb) and place them in the two wall frames above the low walls. This will finish the god peek.
12. The rest of the building can be finished as you see fit—for example, with embrasures, windows, double doors, and ladder hatches.
13. To finish the base, just add as many instant-reload turrets as you need.

## Tower 2
![](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/56f03782-f35e-4ec1-af22-186ff89ef191)

*This tower has 12 god peeks 1st floor and 12 air peeks on the 2nd floor.*

---
This tower follows the same steps as Tower 1, but has a hexagon shape. It offers more peeks, but it takes more time to build. I will only describe the first steps, as the rest of the tower is similar to Tower 1. 
1. Build 5 triangle foundations in one direction, then turn right or left and build another 5 foundations until you return to the first foundation. **(step 1)**
2. It should look like a hexagon now. Fill the empty space with foundations.
3. Build an entry in the middle of the hexagon and place your TC. You can again build up 3 floors with walls, and fill all the empty foundations with wall frames 3 floors up. **(step 2)**
4. Now fill the hexagon with floor tiles.
5. Place two triangle floors on each corner of the hexagon. These will be the "god peeks." **(step 3)**
6. From here on, its the same process as tower 1. You can follow the pictures from here. 

**TIP:** Use /sethome "name" to set a new teleport point for yourself. This will allow you to teleport to your tower any time.
**TIP2:** Use the TC auto upgrade system, to upgrade your base. This saves you time!

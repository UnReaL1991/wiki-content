# Tackling Bradleys

## Overview
Bradleys are significantly easier than patrol helicopters, so there is no strict gear equipment. With good positioning and steady damage, they can be cleared consistently even with standard weapons.

## Recommended Approach
1. Take cover behind the side walls of the bradley islands.
2. Peek carefully and slowly chip down the bradley’s health.
3. Avoid staying exposed for too long as bradley rockets still deal DMG.

## FASTER OPTIONS
Ashmaker or Huff-N-Puff (Legendary Launchers) will destroy bradleys much quicker than normal means.
A normal rocket launcher is still perfectly fine for early difficulties.
## How to start
1. Use /hub.
2. Enter one of the red portals (raid 1-4).
3. Move towards one of the bradley islands.
4. Open the store with /s.
5. Go to the buyable bradley section and purchase one.
## The bradley will drop from the sky
You cannot damage it while airborne, start attacking as soon as it hits the ground, you can also “pre-fire” the landing if you have a Huff-N-Puff.

## Loot rules & Tips
- Only YOU can open the crates.
- Other players can only pick up the items on the ground.
- After opening the crates, they will disappear and drop loot on ground.

TIP: Take what you need quickly to avoid losing items when they drop.
You can also switch raid servers to avoid the cooldowns.
